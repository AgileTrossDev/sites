Tommy Dick Jr. Cancer Foundation

Thomas Joseph Dick Jr., known to us all as Tommy, lost his gallant battle with testicular cancer after three years of treatment on October 18, 2007. Tommy's family began the Tommy Dick Jr. Cancer Foundation to honor his wish to continue the fight against cancer through clinical research for a cure, as well as, provide support for cancer patients and their families. In the years past, the Tommy Dick Jr. Cancer Foundation has hosted Golf Tournaments to raise funds for cancer research and helped families of cancer patients afford to travel to cancer treatment centers for specialized care.

10 Year Anniversary Benefit

2017 marks the ten year anniversary of Tommy's passing. The Tommy Dick Jr. Cancer foundation is hosting a 10 Year Anniversary Benefit on November 11, 2017 at Piccadilly Public House and Restaurant with a fundraising goal of $10,000.00 for 2017. All donations will benefit the Tommy Dick Jr. Cancer Foundation and be donated for cancer research and cancer patient support. If you are unable to be with us at the event and wish to contribute to Tommy's cause, here is your homebase for continuing this brave young man's fight against the devastating disease that has touched so many of our lives.
